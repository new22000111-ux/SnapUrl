PRIVACY POLICY

SnapURL - Fast, Lightweight URL Shortener

1. Introduction

Welcome to SnapURL. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy. SnapURL is a lightweight Android URL shortener application designed with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding information collection and usage.

2. No Data Collection

SnapURL does not collect, store, or process any personal information from its users. We do not create user accounts, require registration, or gather identifying data. Your usage of SnapURL remains completely anonymous.

3. API Integration

SnapURL utilizes the is.gd API for URL shortening functionality. When you shorten a URL, the application sends the link to is.gd's servers for processing. Neither SnapURL nor is.gd maintains logs of user activities or personal information. The shortened URL service operates without tracking user behavior.

4. Permissions

SnapURL requires only one permission: internet access. This permission is exclusively used to communicate with the is.gd API for URL shortening. We do not access any other data on your device, including contacts, location, files, or personal information.

5. No Analytics or Tracking

SnapURL contains zero tracking mechanisms, analytics systems, or telemetry tools. We do not monitor how you use the application, what URLs you shorten, or any other behavioral data. Your privacy is absolute.

6. Open Source and Auditable

SnapURL is 100% open source under the AGPL-3.0 license. The complete source code is publicly available for inspection. Any developer can review the code to verify that no tracking, data collection, or malicious activities occur. We encourage security-conscious users to audit our code.

7. Security

Communication between SnapURL and is.gd API uses secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS). Your shortened URLs are created securely, and no sensitive information is transmitted insecurely.

8. Third-Party Services

SnapURL only integrates with the is.gd URL shortening service. is.gd does not track users or store personal information. For details about is.gd's practices, please visit their website.

9. Children's Privacy

SnapURL is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for children's privacy.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be published to this page with an updated date. Continued use of SnapURL following any updates indicates acceptance of the modified policy.

11. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us. Your privacy is our priority, and we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection and user privacy.
